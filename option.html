About App

This App is meant to help students offering the course: GST in Nigerian Universities. The name of the app does not limit to only Federal university Lafia though some features are.
The app also uses internet connecton to fetch its contents at first launch and later stores the contents on users phone.
It also provides some other functions that require use of internet such as sending of feedback to developer and also checking for newer update.
